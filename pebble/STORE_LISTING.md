AI Daily Digest brings a curated Top 15 list of the most relevant AI updates to your wrist each day.

What you get:
- Daily ranked feed of important AI posts
- Signals across research, releases, benchmarks, and tooling
- Fast on-watch browsing
- Open selected links on your phone instantly
- Video-post awareness (with timeline highlights in backend digest)

Designed for low-noise, high-signal reading.
