# Privacy Policy — AI Daily Digest

AI Daily Digest does not require account login inside the watch app.

## Data processed
- The app fetches a daily digest JSON payload from your configured server URL.
- Selected item links may be opened on your phone browser.

## Data storage
- The watch app does not store personal profile data.
- Any server-side logging/storage is controlled by the server operator.

## Third parties
- Linked content may point to third-party websites (for example, X or blog pages).
